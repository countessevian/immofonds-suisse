Check out these FAQs on SRI

Still deciding if we’re right for you?
These frequently asked questions may help:

Say less. How do I get this?

Our diversified and fully automated Socially Responsible portfolio is available for Automated Investing clients. Just choose the Socially Responsible portfolio option in the sign-up process. Our Socially Responsible portfolio is not available for Stock Investing clients.

Wait. Can I make a socially responsible portfolio with a Stock Investing Account?

If you’re already aware of single stocks that express your values, you’re welcome to DIY your SRIs. But if you want the convenience of a fully-managed and expert-built Socially Responsible portfolio, then open an Automated Investment Account. Either way, we’ll help you invest for long-term good.

How do I know your picks are actually, like, socially responsible?

While we pride ourselves on our honesty (and you look especially nice today, BTW), you don’t have to take our word for it. We have a whole whitepaperabout how we chose funds composed of companies that score well for Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) measures including carbon emissions, diversity, and board composition, according to MSCI.But if you just want the overview, click here.

So, there’s a Socially Responsible alternative for everything?

Not quite everything. In some cases, like with Dividend Stocks and Emerging Bonds, we don’t include those asset classes. For other cases, like Municipal Bonds, the Socially Responsible portfolio uses the same fund as our Classic portfolio.

But what if I don’t like your picks?

You do you! You can customize your Socially Responsible portfolio, add and remove funds, and change how much is invested in each fund any time you want. We have hundreds of additional funds you can choose from, including a variety of SRI funds covering renewable energy, minority empowerment, gender diversity, and more.

Is this like how organic strawberries cost twice as much as regular?

Not at all! The weighted average annual expense ratio of the investments in our Socially Responsible portfolio is still between 0.09-0.18%, and we have the same, ultra-low 0.25% annual advisory fee for both our Classic and Socially Responsible portfolios.
